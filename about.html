About Me

Aspiring Creative Technologist with a Passion for Design-Driven Innovation

I’m a student at Parsons School of Design studying Design and Technology with a deep interest in the intersection of technology, psychology, and design. I’m extremely intrigued by how we interact with different systems, emotionally, cognitively, and physically, and as a designer, how I can shape these different interactions in meaningful ways

My practices stretch a wide range of disciplines, such as physical computing, UI/UX design, installation work, and creative coding. I love to experiment with both the analog and digital worlds, bringing them together to create media that can invoke emotion and inspire connection. Whether I'm prototyping a user experience or creating an immersive installation, I begin every project with curiosity and a desire to spark engagement.
